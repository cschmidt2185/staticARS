Who We Are

A fresh breath on contracting

Founded in 1925 in a small garage in Urbana, Illinois, A&R Mechanical Contractors embarked on a journey fueled by boundless vision and unwaivering determination. With nearly a century of experieince, we have evolved into a fourth-generation, family-owned, service-driven contractor that places the utmost value on innovation and excellence.
