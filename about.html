About A&R Services

Building Excellence Since 1925

Founded in 1925 in a small garage in Urbana, Illinois, A&R Mechanical Contractors has grown into a leading force in the construction and mechanical services industry. Our journey spans nearly a century of dedication, innovation, and unwavering commitment to quality.

As a fourth-generation, family-owned business, we understand the importance of maintaining strong relationships with our clients, employees, and community. Our success is built on a foundation of trust, expertise, and the ability to adapt to changing industry demands while maintaining our core values.

Today, A&R Services continues to evolve and grow, embracing new technologies and sustainable practices while holding true to our traditional values of hard work, integrity, and exceptional service. Our team of skilled professionals brings decades of combined experience to every project, ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety.
